Riyadh College of Technology

Category: Architecture and Building Engineering

Discipline: Educational

Client: General Organization for Technical Education & Vocational Training

Project Description: This project is a new campus for providing technical educaiton and vocational training for a total of 4,330 students. The new complex will integrate the existing electronics institute and a new adjacent site. The complex will encompass nine academic departments (each with its own labs, classrooms and workshops), central academic facilities (library, lecture theaters, computer center), centreal support facilities (general administration, cafeterias, student center, mosque) maintenance facilities, student accommodation and sports facilities. The site area (excluding electronics institute ) is 196,800 sq.m. Gross built-area on new site is approximately 107,390 sq.m. Area of existing facilities of the electronic institute to be integrated in the new campus is approx. 57,000 sq.m.
