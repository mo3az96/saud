Architecture and Building Engineering

Le Corbusier said: "You employ stone, wood, and concrete, and with these materials you build houses and palaces; that is construction. Ingenuity is at work. But suddenly you touch my heart, you do me good. I am happy and l say: "This is beautiful. That is architecture".

A front runner in architecture and building engineering, Saudconsult has undertaken a vast number of projects and demonstrated excellent skills in designing commercial, residential, educational, healthcare and public buildings in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and beyond, from Lebanon, Yemen, and Sudan to Sierra Leone, Mali, Djibouti, Azerbaijan, and others.

With services from construction and construction supervision to tender evaluation and feasibility studies, Saudconsult's scope of work in the building engineering domain is comprehensive and covers structural, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC (Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning) systems design.

Saudconsult is behind the design and construction of some of the kingdom's most prestigious structures. Among them are the Ministry of Planning Building in Jeddah, Tabuk Emirate Complex, Qassim Emirate Complex, Fire Station, Safa Souq and Water Tower/Revolving Restaurant at Al-Kharj, Dammam Water Tower, Taif Water Tower and Restaurant, the Riyadh Courts Complex, and a variety of housing and commercial-type facilities in the Diplomatic Quarters of Riyadh. Some of Saudconsult's structures have gained wide acclaim from international professional circles.
